How to record a show for WolfPack Radio

How to make a show for Wolfpack Radio.

Time: 1 hour and a half to 3 hours
Awesome rating: 100% Awesome!!!

If you’re interested in joining Wolfpack Radio and want to know more about how to make a show for us, follow the article that I’ve provided for you.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Hindenburg Application
  • Computer
  • Microphone to Record
  • Headset
  • Clean Music of your choice
  • DJ Board

  1. Start Up

    Log in to the WolfPack Radio computer and start up Hindenburg and your DJ Board. Connect your device on which you’re playing music and connect it to Hindenburg. Once you have the device of your choice, connect it to Rodecaster Pro. Specific details will be provided in the link. If you are recording with a guest over the phone, you can use multiple inputs. Everything that comes out of the device will be recorded, so turn off notifications, refrain from watching videos, etc.

  2. Microphone

    Plug into headphone input “1” at the back of the board. You can adjust headphone monitoring levels with the upper right knob beneath the headphone symbol and the overhead monitors with the knob beneath the speaker symbol at the far right. When recording your voice, turn the overhead monitors down to avoid feedback.

    Close up of Microphone

  3. Recording

    You may begin recording when all your inputs are set to the correct level, and the tracks are armed. Start the recording by clicking on the blinking red circle in the upper middle of the window. When you are done recording, click the square “stop” button next to the recording button to stop recording. Make sure to save your work again, “File > Save.”

  4. Editing

    There are a number of editing styles and you don’t need to follow the formula that we provide. Drag and alternate your songs between tracks, and overlap them slightly to fade them together and avoid silent space. You can fade tracks by clicking on an upper corner of an audio section and dragging it towards the song’s center. If needed, you should adjust the levels on your tracks. If required -- you should have recorded close to the correct and consistent level, but it may be necessary to even out tracks. Levels can be adjusted for the whole track using the speaker slider at the left of the track or for individual songs by selecting a section and clicking and dragging the small rectangle in the top middle of the section up or down.

  5. Exporting

    When you are satisfied with your show, save the Hindenburg file one more time. To export, click File > Export…, and select the SPRING 2022 folder as the location. Set format to .mp3 with the format dropdown. (Ensure that the Encoding dropdown is set to Med Quality). Open the Chrome browser and select the first bookmark to open the Drive folder (WPR Show Uploads Spring 2022).
